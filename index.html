How to use this tool

Five steps to a finished, embeddable GPCR tree animation.

  1. Scroll to preview. Scroll down the page to watch the tree grow from its centre. Keep scrolling and the named tip highlights appear at the end.
  2. Label your tips. Click Label tips in the top-left, then click any branch tip and type the GPCR name (e.g. ADRB1). Click an existing tip to edit, recolour, or delete it.
  3. Pick your colours. Click Colors to change the tree, tip points, label text, and background. Changes are saved automatically as you adjust them.
  4. Generate the embed. Click Embed when you're happy. A self-contained HTML snippet appears — hit Copy to clipboard.
  5. Paste into Webflow. Add a Webflow Embed element where you want the animation to appear, paste the snippet, save, and publish. The tree will animate as visitors scroll the page.
